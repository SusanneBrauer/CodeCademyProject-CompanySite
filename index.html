About BlackTree Studio

BlackTree Studios are located just outside Bromsgrove in the beautiful Worcestershire countryside, incorporating: BlackTree Recording, BlackTree Mobile & BlackTree Video Productions. Established in 2012 & consists of a large live room, medium isolation area & vocal booth.

The live room has an open plan layout which is large enough for full band live multitrack sessions with nice sound separation & good visual communication. It is also a great drum tracking room offering a big room & dry room recording options.

BlackTree offers an idyllic recording & filming environment, situated just 5 miles from the M5 & M42 motorways.
